Extensions

Posted on October 1st, 2007 by Ethan

These are the extensions that I use on a regular basis.

  • AdBlockPlus - Say goodbye to most ads. Updates it's own blacklist. Works so well that you start to forget how many ads are normally on a webpage, until you use a browser without it.

  • All-In-One Gestures - Keeps you from having to click so many buttons on the navigation bar.

  • ColorZilla - Get hex and RGB values for any color on a website, eyedropper style.

  • ConQuery - Highlight text and then search it to any of your search plug-ins from right-click context menu.

  • Download Statusbar - Firefox's download manager kind of sucks.

  • FireFTP - FTP from your browser. Pretty good interface too.

  • Forecastfox - Weather.

  • FoxyTunes - Control your media player from Firefox, meaning you don't have to alt-tab so much.

  • Gmail Manager - Inbox notification.

  • Google Browser Sync - Synchs bookmarks and sessions across multiple computers in any number of locations. Absolutely great. I close by browser on my desktop at home when I leave, and when I open it in my laptop at work I've got the same pages waiting for me.

  • MinimizeToTray - Windows only, keeps the taskbar less cluttered.

  • Plain Text to Link - Highlight a plain text URL, and right-click to open it like a real link. Easier than copy-pasting.

  • Tab Mix Plus - Lots of little tweaks for tab behavior. Possibly only for the neurotic.
