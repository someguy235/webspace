ZipSkinny

Posted on October 24, 2007 by Ethan

See how your zip code stacks up to others in your area. This page uses US census information to compare education, marital status, income, and occupation data. 28804 in north Asheville - where the university is - has the highest education rate (as you might expect) and highest median income (which you probably wouldn't) in the city. But we also have low marriage rates and in general haven't lived here very long.

Baby Names

Fly Clear

Posted on October 11th, 2007 by Ethan

Tired of being hassled by airport security? Willing to hand over all of your personal and biometric information and $99.95/year to a third-party security company? Well then this is the product for you. Clear is offering a card that certifies that you have undergone a TSA background check, and that you are not a wanted terrorist. For this you get to skip to the front of the security line and submit to a retinal scan in selected airports. Possibly convenient, definitely creepy.
